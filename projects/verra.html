VERRA
With an intuitive and clean design, the integrated tool offers a seamless  tender deal creation process for a specific financial service category in the company.

The primary objective of this project was to create a user-friendly application that simplifies the complex process of deal creation with external company products and quoting. The application allows the company to have an all in one quoting tool for all internal and external products in one single quote. By automating critical financial calculations and integrating essential business data, the tool aims to enhance accuracy and efficiency, enabling users to quickly and easily generate comprehensive quotes.

Key functionalities include automatic product price generation,  the ability to customise customer financial CAPEX, revenue post-discount projections, and profit calculations.

The integrated quoting and deal creation tool has significantly improved the efficiency and accuracy of the company's financial service category's quoting processes. The tool's intuitive design and comprehensive feature set enable users to quickly generate detailed quotes, enhancing both internal workflow and customer satisfaction.

This project highlights my capability to deliver customized solutions that meet specific business needs, combining design sensibility with technical expertise to drive business value.
