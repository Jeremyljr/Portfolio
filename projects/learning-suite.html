Content Management System
Learning-Suite
The Learning Suite
Custom-built CMS that is both user-friendly & minimal looking. Achieving greater efficiency, better collaboration, and enhanced productivity in the team.

This project focused on designing and developing a custom-built Content Management System (CMS) that is both user-friendly and visually minimalistic, equipped with integrations that support the team's specific needs. The CMS was meticulously designed to offer a clean, uncluttered interface that emphasizes ease of use and intuitive navigation.

The primary goal was to create a platform that enhances team efficiency, fosters better collaboration, and significantly improves productivity. The CMS serves as a central hub for managing content and streamlining workflows within the team. The user-friendly design ensures that even those with minimal technical expertise can manage content effortlessly, contributing to overall team productivity.

The project commenced with a thorough needs assessment, where I worked closely with the team to understand their specific requirements and pain points. Based on these insights, I developed initial design system components, concepts and wireframes that emphasized user-friendly approach. Once the design was approved, I proceeded with the development phase, building the frontend components on Webflow. Rigorous testing was conducted to ensure the CMS met all functional and performance criteria, followed by user training sessions to facilitate smooth adoption.

By delivering a custom-built CMS, this project showcases my ability to create tailored solutions that address specific business challenges, leveraging both design and technical expertise to deliver results.
